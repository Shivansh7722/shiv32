Our Story & Mission

Family First Chiropractic was founded in 2010 by Dr. Michael Smith with a simple vision: to provide compassionate, family-centered chiropractic care that addresses the root causes of health issues rather than just symptoms.

After experiencing the transformative power of chiropractic care firsthand following a sports injury in college, Dr. Smith dedicated his career to helping families experience the same life-changing benefits.

"We believe every family deserves access to natural, drug-free healthcare that supports the body's incredible ability to heal itself."

— Dr. Michael Smith
